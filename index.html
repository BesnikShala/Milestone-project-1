About Us

Here at Prestige Automotive, we have a combined 20 year experience in the car industry. We aim to provide key services which are designed to make your vehicle more personalised towards you and your taste. Whether you want to change the look or you simply want that new car feel we have a range of services for you to choose from. We provide a warpping service which not only changes the colour and texture of the car but it also provides a protection to your original paint work. The materials used are from recycled materials and do not damage the environment. The detailing service is there to ensure you get that new car feel everytime you visit us, we will target the most affected areas and even areas which you did not think can be cleaned. Our services range from both external and internal deep cleansing to provide that new car look and feel. thanks to our ever growing community we have also introduced our chip tune service which provides a power boost to your engine.

About Us

Where We Began . . .

Here at Prestige Automotive we do not forget where we started. With nothing but a dream, a dingey garage and a bag full of tools we started on this journey to provide good a good and honest service. We wanted to turn our passion into a business, we stuck to what we know best which is good customer service and product knowledge. Soon word of mouth spread and we were soon struggling to meet demand. The only way forward was to invest in new commercial space and equipment!

Where We Are Now . . .

We have come a long way since our humble beginnings. We have a 5000sq feet unit which houses our three perfected services which include car wrapping, detailing and engine tuning. We have a collected 20 years of experience which means we aim to provide the best possible service based on our knowledge over the years. We provide quality guarantee with all our services to make sure our customers are satisfied.
